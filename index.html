Where is NDIS funding most used and for accessing what services?

Section 1 Heading

  • The line chart shows the steady increase in NDIS funding from 2019 to 2024.
  • 2023 saw a slight dip in funding before a rise again in 2024.
  • The bar chart indicates Therapy and Support Coordination are the most accessed services in 2024.

Section 2 Heading

  • New South Wales has the highest number of people accessing NDIS funding with over 3,200 individuals.
  • Victoria and Queensland follow closely, reflecting a high demand in the eastern states.
  • Northern Territory and ACT show the lowest access to funding, consistent with their lower population densities.
